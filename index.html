Hey there, welcome to my personal website!

I’m a Ph.D. candidate at McGill University in the Atmospheric Radiation Group (AtmRad). My research focuses on improving our understanding of climate change from a radiative perspective.

I use spectrally resolved longwave (infrared) measurements to analyze trends, detect cloud-related changes, and retrieve key atmospheric variables such as temperature and humidity profiles from radiation data.

Besides being passionate about climate, physics, and radiation, I also enjoy spending time outdoors biking, climbing, and hiking.
